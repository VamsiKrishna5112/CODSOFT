🧠 How it Works

Haar Cascade Classifier: This algorithm uses a cascade of classifiers trained on positive and negative images. It scans the image at different scales to detect faces by identifying features like edges and corners that are characteristic of faces.

Real-time Detection: The system processes video frames or images in real-time, drawing bounding boxes around detected faces and labeling them for easy identification.
